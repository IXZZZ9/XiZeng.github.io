## Active Projects

### GradES: Gradient-Based Early Stopping for Transformers
**Status**: Submitted to MLsys 2026 | **Role**: Co-First Author

Developed a training optimization method achieving significant speedups in transformer training through gradient monitoring.

**Key Contributions**:
- Significant reduction in training time without performance degradation
- Comprehensive experimental validation across architectures
- Open-source implementation released on GitHub, PyPI, and Hugging Face

**Resources**: [arXiv:2509.01842](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.01842)

**Technical Stack**: PyTorch, Transformers

### Interpretable Silent Speech Recognition
**Collaboration**: Center for Intelligent Medical Electronics, Fudan University | **Status**: Manuscript in preparation

Developed an interpretable framework for silent speech recognition using high-density sEMG signals with reduced hardware requirements.

**Key Innovations**:
- Electrode importance mapping using Deep Forest's MDI interpretability
- Significant performance with reduced electrode sets
- Statistical validation of interpretability approach

**Impact**: Cost-effective deployment for assistive technology

**Technical Stack**: Python, scikit-learn, Deep Forest
