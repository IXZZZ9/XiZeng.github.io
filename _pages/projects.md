## Current Projects

### ActionRes: Addressing Catastrophic Forgetting through Action-Space Composition
**Status**: Active Development | **Role**: Lead Researcher

A novel framework connecting delta actions with catastrophic forgetting dynamics in sequential task learning. This project investigates fundamental questions about knowledge preservation in continual learning systems.

**Key Contributions**:
- First systematic analysis of action-space composition for forgetting mitigation
- Novel residual architecture design preserving learned representations
- Theoretical framework distinguishing knowledge accessibility from destruction

**Technical Stack**: PyTorch, NumPy, Weights & Biases

---

### GradES: Gradient-Based Early Stopping for Transformers
**Status**: Submitted to ICLR 2026 | **Role**: Co-First Author

Developed an innovative training optimization method achieving significant speedups in transformer training through intelligent gradient monitoring.

**Key Achievements**:
- 30-50% reduction in training time without performance degradation
- Comprehensive experimental validation across multiple architectures
- Open-source release with extensive documentation

**Resources**:
- [arXiv Preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.01842)

## Completed Projects

### Interpretable Silent Speech Recognition with Deep Forest
**Collaboration**: Center for Intelligent Medical Electronics, Fudan University | **Role**: Lead ML Engineer

Developed an interpretable framework for silent speech recognition using high-density sEMG signals, focusing on practical deployment with reduced hardware requirements.

**Key Innovations**:
- Electrode importance mapping using Mean Decrease in Impurity (MDI)
- Demonstrated 40% electrode reduction with maintained accuracy
- Statistical validation of interpretability approach

**Impact**: Enables cost-effective deployment of silent speech interfaces for assistive technology

**Technical Stack**: scikit-learn, Deep Forest, Statistical Analysis Tools

---

### Robust Keypoint Detection under Occlusion
**Framework**: Google MediaPipe Enhancement | **Role**: Research Engineer

Enhanced MediaPipe's pose estimation capabilities for robust performance under partial occlusion using Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA).

**Achievements**:
- 25% improvement in detection accuracy under occlusion
- 10x faster adaptation compared to full fine-tuning
- Model size suitable for mobile deployment

**Applications**: AR/VR interfaces, gesture recognition, accessibility tools

**Technical Stack**: TensorFlow Lite, MediaPipe, LoRA

---

### Atherosclerosis Risk Prediction System
**Collaboration**: Fudan University Medical Center | **Role**: ML Researcher

Developed predictive models for early atherosclerosis detection using multivariate health metrics from clinical data.

**Key Contributions**:
- Handled severely imbalanced medical datasets (1:50 ratio)
- Achieved 0.89 AUC with rigorous cross-validation
- Statistical significance testing for clinical validity

**Clinical Impact**: System under evaluation for integration into screening protocols

**Technical Stack**: Python, pandas, scikit-learn, R (statistical analysis)
